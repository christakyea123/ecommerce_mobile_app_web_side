Consumer Health Data Privacy

Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This policy describes how Glomek handles consumer health data in connection with health-related products.

Health Data We May Collect

When you purchase health products, we may process purchase preference data. We do not collect medical records, diagnoses, or treatment information.

How We Protect Health Data

  • Health purchase data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access limited to authorized personnel
  • We never sell health-related data to third parties
  • Data retention limited as required by law

Contact Us

For health data privacy questions: privacy@glomek.com
